All the information regarding COVID-19 + Helpful Features in one place!

Throughout the past 3 months, there has been a lot of different information regarding COVID-19 spreading through news media outlets. That was until now, with the COVID-19 State Tracker, one can easily find out important pieces of information regarding the COVID-19 Situation in their respective state whilst being able to chat with an IBM Watson Bot with any questions/concerns they may have and browse through important news regarding COIVD-19 in their state. Lastly, I added a face-touch collision detector, meaning you will be notified if your hands make contact with your face whilst the tab is open in order to lessen the spread of germs!
